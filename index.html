Yazaan

Yazaan - the youth azaan is a call to the youth to shed their inhibitions and tame the wild energy of technology that is growing fiercer every passing day

Yazaan's first edition launched in March 2013 solely by MCA with a promise that, soon we will Leap Out Of Bounds and included Masters of Technology and Masters of Busniess Administration streams and then we have presented the second edition of Yazaan which proudly calls itself as a first Post-Graduate Magazine of NIT Durgapur.

Yazaan's 2013 edition said Leap Out Of Bounds, Yazaan's 2014 edition is saying leaping out of bounds with a promise that the day will never come when Yazaan's 20XX edition says leapt out of bounds as the hard work and dedication poured in by each and every Post-Graduate batches will be transfered to the forthcoming generation.
